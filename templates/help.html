Help

This app lets users track their movies or shows in one collection.

  • To find the specific media, select Add OMDb on the navigation bar.
  • If your specific media isn’t available when search, select Manual Add on the navigation bar to add it manually.
  • To remove a media, hover over the media in the home page. Click on the check mark on the top left of the media’s cover to remove.
