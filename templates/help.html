Help

This app lets users track their various forms of media in one collection.

  • To find the specific media, select Search on the navigation bar.
  • If your specific media isn’t available when search, you can add it manually. The option to add manually is present in the search page.
  • To remove a media, select the media in either the home page or via search. Click on the check mark on the top left of the media’s cover to remove.
